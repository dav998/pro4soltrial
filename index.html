ALBERTO NOGALES

ROAD AND INFRASTRUCTURE PROJECTS SPECIALIST

Alberto Nogales Quintanilla.

Alberto holds a Masters degree in Civil Engineering from Stanford University and an Urban & Regional Planning Specialty from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT). He is an international consultant with 25 years of professional experience on large and complex transport infrastructure projects, primarily in the construction and maintenance of roads & highway networks. Alberto, based in Washington DC, USA worked in the identification, preparation, and evaluation of transport and urban development projects in more than 45 countries. He is currently interested project infrastructure financing & public investment, municipal development, and institutional strengthening of Sub-National & Local Governments. Alberto has extensive experience in various regions including Latin America, South Asia, Sub- Saharan Africa, and East Asia working as a Consultant for International Development organizations such as the World Bank, the Inter-American Development Bank (IADB) and the African Development Bank.

JULIO GALINDO

CADASTER, TOPOGRAPHY & MINING SPECIALIST

Julio A. Galindo Queralt.

Julio has a B.S. in Civil Engineering with a specialty in Structural Design from the Escuela de Ingenieria (EMI) (La Paz, Bolivia) with a specialty in Satellite Geodesy. Julio has more than 35 years serving as a structural engineer in the fields of design, calculation, supervision and construction. He is a leading expert in satellite geodesic measurement in real time for oil and gas pipelines and digital terrestrial photogrammetry for refineries. Currently, he is involved in mining exploration, cadaster applications, 3D Computer Numerical Control Fabrication, and Deep Drilling. Julio is an innovator, inventor, and manufacturer of drilling machines, and instruments for stress measurement, and rock fracturing tools for pressures up to 420 MPa. For decades, he has held positions as Executive Director and Director of Development of three companies with international presence in Chile, Argentina, and Bolivia.

MARIO GALINDO

STRUCTURES & ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE SPECIALIST

Mario A. Galindo Queralt.

Mario has a B.S. in Civil Engineering from the Escuela de Ingenieria (Bolivia). He also has obtained a M.S. in Numerical Methodologies; a Ph.D. in Roads, Canals, & Ports Engineering from the Universidad Politécnica de Cataluña (Spain); and a Specialization in Deep Learning Artificial Intelligence (AI) from Stanford University (USA). Mario is currently a professor of Mechanics of Medium Mechanics, Resistence of Materials, Structural Analysis, Mathematics, and Coding Languges C & C++. He has over 37 years experience as an engineer in structural design and geodesy, and as a researcher in Numerical Methods, Finite Elements, and Material Fracturing. Mario has designed large-scale structures, detection & solution of structural pathologies, and developed numerical models in ​​Geodesy. He is currently researching the Use of Deep Recurrent Neural Networks in AI for the prediction of future events in non-periodic semi-random problems.

JORGE ABELA

PROJECT MANAGMENT SPECIALIST

Jorge Abela Gisbert

Jorge has a B.S. in Electronic Systems Engineer from the Escuela de Ingenieria (EMI) (La Paz, Bolivia) and an MBA from the Universidad Catolica (La Paz, Bolivia). He also has a Digital Transformation Diploma from the Universidad del Desarrollo (Santiago, Chile). Over 30 years of experience of executive leadership, in designing, and implementing public & private sector projects including electronic projects in the health, manufacturing, telecommunications, and energy sectors. Jorge has experience in complex project assessment, development, implementation, monitoring, and evaluation. Currently, Jorge works in strategic thinking methodologies and digital transformation initiatives to deliver outstanding results in public, NGO, and private business organizations. He possesses solid knowledge in fundraising, requests for proposals, project portfolio management, and project implementation & monitoring within the international cooperation organizations including the Inter-American Development Bank (IADB) and the US Agency for International Development (USAID).

MARCELO ALFARO

PAVEMENT SPECIALIST

Fernando J. Marcelo Alfaro Santiago.

Marcelo has an MS in Civil Engineer from Bradford University (England). He is a Certified Instructor for the National Highway Institute (NHI), and Certified Concrete Construction Inspector by the American Concrete Institute (ACI). Since 2000, he serves as the General Manager of the Bolivian Cement & Concrete Institute. Marcelo’s previous roles included Technical Manager, Construction Supervisor, and Professional Engineer. He has developed courses for design, construction and rehabilitation of Portland cement concrete, soil-cement, concrete technology, and the Rigid and Flexible Pavement Design Software (DIPAV). He currently teaches roads and highways post-graduate students at the Universidad Mayor de San Andres (La, Paz, Bolivia) and Structural Design at the Universidad San Francisco Xavier (Sucre, Bolivia). Marcelo is an expert on Portland cement concrete, he has advised and led the technical design on more than 50 highway pavement projects in South America.

JAIME PAREDES

PROJECT EVALUATION SPECIALIST

Jaime R. Paredes Verástegui.

Jaime has a B.S. in Electronic Systems Engineering from the Escuela de Ingenieria (La Paz, Bolivia) and an M.S. in Management and Public Policies, from the Universidad Catolica (La Paz, Bolivia). Currently, he is a Professor in projects formulation, financial, economic, and social evaluation in several academic institutions, including the Universidad Catolica, and the School of Engineering. Jaime has over 35 years of experience as an engineer working in the application of electronic systems and nuclear energy in medicine. Jaime has been a consultant for international development organizations and has extensive experience in the evaluation and implementation of public and private investment projects. He has been involved in the development of technical & methodological tools for the preparation and evaluation of projects. Jaime currently advises post-graduate students on the application of problem solving and project development in multiple fields.
