ROAD AND INFRASTRUCTURE PROJECTS SPECIALIST

Alberto Nogales Quintanilla.

Owner & Founder Director - Transport Infrastructure & Urban Development.
Masters degree in Civil Engineering at Stanford University and an Urban & Regional Planning Specialty from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT). International consultant with 25 years of professional experience on large and complex infrastructure projects, primarily in the construction & maintenance of roads & highways.
Based in Washington DC, USA worked in the identification, preparation, and evaluation of transport and urban development projects in more than 45 countries. He has extensive experience in various regions including Latin America, South Asia, Sub-Saharan Africa, and East Asia working as a Consultant for International Development organizations like the World Bank, the Inter-American Development Bank and the African Development Bank.

JULIO GALINDO

CADASTER, TOPOGRAPHY & MINING SPECIALIST

Julio A. Galindo Queralt.

Structural Engineer with a specialty in Satellite Geodesy.
Experience: 35 years as a polytechnic engineer in the fields of design, calculation, supervision and construction; satellite geodesic measurement in real time for oil and gas pipelines; digital terrestrial photogrammetry for refineries, mining exploration and cadaster applications.
Innovator, inventor and manufacturer of: drilling machines, instruments for stress measurement and rock fracturing up to pressures of 420 MPa.
